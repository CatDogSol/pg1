Name the Penguin Chick

Donate to help choose the name of our new African penguin chick!

Vote Today!

You can help choose the new African penguin chick's name. Our animal care team has selected three meaningful name options, Jupiter, Pluto, and Sol, and now it’s your turn to decide which one wins.

Fun fact: the chick’s siblings are named Moon, Titan, and Calypso.

How It Works

1️⃣ Choose your favorite penguin chick name
2️⃣ Donate to cast your vote
3️⃣ The name with the highest donation total wins
4️⃣ Vote by Tuesday, April 28 at 11:59 PM CT. The winning name will be announced the following day.

Every vote supports animal care and conservation at Audubon Nature Institute.
